How it works

From soil sample to recommendation

OptiCrop was trained on agricultural records covering 22 crop varieties. The pipeline cleans and scales each soil/climate reading, then a Logistic Regression classifier — benchmarked against K-Nearest Neighbors and K-Means clustering during development — predicts the crop with the highest likelihood of thriving under your conditions, along with the runner-up matches.

Built with Python, scikit-learn & Flask — OptiCrop Smart Agricultural Production Optimization Engine
